Our Facilities

Modern infrastructure for quality education

School Infrastructure

VKS Public School boasts excellent infrastructure designed to provide a conducive learning environment. Our facilities meet all standards prescribed by the education board and are continuously upgraded to enhance the learning experience.

School Building

Our school has a private building with pucca boundary wall, ensuring safety and security. The building includes:

  • 39 Classrooms: All in good condition, spacious and well-ventilated
  • 2 Additional Rooms: For non-teaching activities
  • Separate Room: For Headmaster/Teacher
  • Electric Connection: Throughout the premises

Sanitation Facilities

We maintain excellent sanitation facilities to ensure hygiene and comfort for all students:

  • 5 Boys Toilets: All functional and clean
  • 5 Girls Toilets: All functional and clean
  • Drinking Water: Available through functional hand pumps
  • Regular Cleaning: Maintained throughout the day

Library

Our well-stocked library is a treasure trove of knowledge with:

  • 1200+ Books: Covering various subjects and genres
  • Reference Section: For in-depth study and research
  • Reading Room: Quiet space for students to study
  • Newspapers & Magazines: For current affairs and general knowledge

Computer Lab

Our computer lab provides students with hands-on experience in technology:

  • 25 Computers: All functional for teaching and learning
  • Internet Access: For research and learning purposes
  • Software: Educational and productivity software installed
  • Supervision: By qualified computer teachers

Playground

Our playground supports physical development and sports activities:

  • Spacious Area: For various sports and activities
  • Sports Equipment: For cricket, football, volleyball, and more
  • Annual Sports Day: Showcasing students' athletic talents
  • Physical Education: Regular classes for all grades
