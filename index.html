Welcome to VKS Public School

Established in 1992, V.K.S. Public School, Majhola is a premier educational institution located in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh. We provide quality education from Grade 1 to 12 with English as the medium of instruction.

Our school is managed by Pvt. Unaided organization and is committed to nurturing young minds through a balanced approach to academics and extracurricular activities.
