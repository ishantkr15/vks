Academics

Our curriculum and academic programs

Academic Programs

VKS Public School offers a comprehensive academic program from Class 1 to Class 12, following the curriculum prescribed by the Uttar Pradesh Board. Our medium of instruction is English, preparing students for global opportunities.

Primary School (Classes 1-5)

Our primary school program focuses on building strong foundations in core subjects while nurturing curiosity and creativity.

Subjects Offered:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies
  • Hindi
  • Art & Craft
  • Music
  • Physical Education

Special Features:

  • Activity-based learning
  • Focus on reading and writing skills
  • Basic computer awareness
  • Regular assessments

Middle School (Classes 6-8)

The middle school curriculum bridges primary education with secondary education, introducing more specialized subjects.

Subjects Offered:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies (History, Geography, Civics)
  • Hindi
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education

Special Features:

  • Project-based learning
  • Introduction to scientific methods
  • Enhanced computer skills
  • Regular assessments and projects

Secondary School (Classes 9-10)

Our secondary program prepares students for the UP Board High School examinations with rigorous academic training.

Subjects Offered:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)
  • Hindi
  • Computer Applications

Special Features:

  • Board exam preparation
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Regular mock tests
  • Doubt clearing sessions

Senior Secondary (Classes 11-12)

We offer three streams in senior secondary to cater to diverse career aspirations of our students.

Science Stream

For students aspiring for careers in engineering, medicine, or pure sciences.

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology/Mathematics
  • English
  • Computer Science/Physical Education

Commerce Stream

For students interested in business, finance, and management careers.

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • English
  • Informatics Practices/Mathematics

Arts Stream

For students interested in humanities, social sciences, and creative fields.

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Geography/Economics
  • English
  • Fine Arts/Psychology

Academic Calendar

Our academic year begins in April and ends in March, divided into two terms:

First Term

April to September

  • Unit Tests in June
  • Half-yearly Examinations in September
  • Summer Vacation: Mid-May to End June

Second Term

October to March

  • Unit Tests in December
  • Annual Examinations in March
  • Winter Vacation: Last week of December

Assessment System

We follow a comprehensive assessment system to evaluate student progress:

Formative Assessment

Regular class tests, quizzes, projects, and assignments throughout the term

Summative Assessment

Term-end examinations covering the entire syllabus

Continuous Evaluation

Assessment of class participation, discipline, and extracurricular activities

About VKS

V.K.S. Public School, Majhola was established in 1992 and is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. Located in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, we provide quality education from Grade 1 to 12.
