About Our School

Learn about our history, mission, and vision

Our History

V.K.S. Public School, Majhola was established in 1992 and is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. Located in the urban area of Moradabad City Boys block of Moradabad district, Uttar Pradesh, our school has been providing quality education for over 30 years.

The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12 and is Co-educational. English is the medium of instructions in our school, preparing students for global opportunities.

Our academic session starts in April each year, following the standard academic calendar of Uttar Pradesh.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to become responsible citizens, critical thinkers, and lifelong learners in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and innovation while preserving cultural values.
